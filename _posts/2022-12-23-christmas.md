## Merry Xmas! 

$$
\huge
\begin{aligned}
&y=\frac{\log _{e}\left(\frac{x}{m}-s a\right)}{r^{2}} \\
&y r^{2}=\log _{e}\left(\frac{x}{m}-s a\right) \\
&e^{y r^{2}}=\frac{x}{m}-s a \\
&m e^{y^{r^{2}}}=x-m s a \\
&m e^{r r y}=x-m a s
\end{aligned}
$$
